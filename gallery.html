Gallary

Faisal Mosque

The Faisal Mosque is the national mosque of Pakistan, located in the capital city, Islamabad.
It is the fifth-largest mosque in the world.

Badshahi Mosque

The Badshahi Mosque is one of the largest mosques in the world.it is an iconic symbol of Mughal architecture and a significant cultural and historical landmark.

Germany

A leading European nation,is renowned for its rich history, cultural heritage,and economic strength.It's known for innovations in technology, engineering, and its vibrant cities like Berlin and Munich.

Italy

Italy's culture is celebrated for its rich history in art, architecture, and music, with a strong emphasis on family and tradition. Its cuisine, fashion, and festivals are renowned worldwide, reflecting its vibrant heritage and lifestyle.

India

India, a culturally diverse nation in South Asia, boasts a rich tapestry of traditions, spirituality, and a burgeoning economy, making it a dynamic blend of ancient wisdom and modern aspirations.

London

London, England's capital, is famous for its historic landmarks, diverse culture, and global influence in finance and the arts.

Scotland

Scotland, part of the United Kingdom, is known for its stunning landscapes, including rugged highlands and historic castles. It has a rich cultural heritage, famous for tartan, bagpipes, and Scotch whisky, alongside vibrant cities like Edinburgh and Glasgow.

New zealand

New Zealand, situated in the southwestern Pacific Ocean, is celebrated for its stunning landscapes, outdoor adventure opportunities, Maori culture, and vibrant cities like Auckland and Wellington.

China

China, a global powerhouse in East Asia, is known for its ancient civilization, rapid economic growth, and cultural landmarks like the Great Wall.

United State

The United States, a diverse nation in North America, is renowned for its cultural influence, economic prowess, and iconic landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty.

Japan

Japan, located in East Asia, is celebrated for its technological innovation, rich cultural heritage, and stunning blend of ancient traditions with modernity.

Russia

Russia, spanning Eastern Europe and Northern Asia,is known for its vast landscapes,rich history and global influence in politics,energy resources and culture.
